# 15M Strategy Research - 2026-03-27

## Task
Research and develop a profitable 15-minute (15M) timeframe trading strategy for BTCUSDT.
Targets: Win Rate ≥ 50%, Profit Factor ≥ 2.0, Max Drawdown ≤ 30%, Sharpe ≥ 1.5

## Data
- Source: `data/btcusdt_15m.parquet`
- Range: 2024-03-26 to 2026-03-26 (70,080 bars, ~2 years)
- ATR: avg 275 points (0.33% of price per 15M bar)

## Result: TARGET CRITERIA NOT MET

The 15M timeframe on BTCUSDT shows very low signal quality for pure technical analysis strategies.
Extensive testing of multiple strategy types all failed to meet targets.

## Best Strategy Found

**Name**: EMA Cross Pullback Long
**Type**: Trend-following, Long-only
**Signal**: EMA(9,21) crossover + Daily EMA20 uptrend + RSI 45-65 + Vol > 1.0x MA

### Parameters
- Stop Loss: 4.0x ATR (~1.3% at current volatility)
- Take Profit: 8.0x ATR (~2.6% at current volatility)
- Max Holding: 10 bars (2.5 hours)

### Performance (full period: 2024-03 to 2026-03)
- Total trades: 252
- Win rate: 43.7%
- Profit factor: 1.06
- Max drawdown: -11.7%
- Sharpe ratio: 0.23
- Cumulative return: +3.75%
- Avg bars held: 9.6
- R/R ratio: 1.37
- Dominant exit: MAX_BARS (87%), SL (9%), TP (4%)

## Key Finding

**15M BTCUSDT is essentially non-tradable with pure technical indicators.**

The core problem:
- ATR = ~0.33% per bar (high volatility relative to timeframe)
- Even with wide SL (4x ATR = ~1.3%), stops get hit by normal volatility
- TP needs to be very wide (8x ATR = ~2.6%) to give trades room
- BUT wide TP rarely gets hit (only 4% of exits)
- Result: most trades exit via MAX_BARS with small wins/losses → R/R ~1.3

**The fundamental issue**: Technical indicators on 15M BTC have no significant predictive edge. The market is too efficient at this timeframe. To succeed at 15M requires:
1. Market microstructure / order flow analysis
2. Machine learning / pattern recognition
3. News/event-based signals
4. Cross-exchange arbitrage

## Recommendations

1. **For 15M trading**: Use strategies from higher timeframes (4H, Daily) as trend filters
2. **For pure 15M**: Consider market-making or arbitrage approaches rather than directional trading
3. **Best parameters found** (even if not meeting targets): sl=4xATR, tp=8xATR, mb=10, long-only with daily trend filter
